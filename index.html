About VELSCI Globals

VELSCI Globals is a trusted textile manufacturer and supplier based in Tamil Nadu, India. We specialize in delivering high-quality textile products for both wholesale and retail customers, meeting global quality standards with precision and consistency. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every product reflects superior craftsmanship and durability.

With years of experience in the textile industry, we pride ourselves on our innovative manufacturing processes and a dedicated team that upholds the highest standards of integrity. We aim to make a significant impact on the textile market by providing sustainable and ethically sourced materials.
